Source: [OpenTriviaQA](https://github.com/uberspot/OpenTriviaQA) (Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International).

The trivia question bank in this project is derived from OpenTriviaQA. The dataset is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0 and includes multiple choice question text and answers.

## Word list (Wordle)
Source: [dwyl/english-words](https://github.com/dwyl/english-words) (Unlicense).

The Wordle word list is filtered from the `words_alpha.txt` list and curated to be less common but still playable.
